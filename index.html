Adithya Prateek Singh Sisodia

Aditya Prateek Singh Sisodia, better known by his stage name Badshah, is an Indian rapper and singer known for his Hindi, Haryanvi, and Punjabi songs. He is also known for being India's highest-paid rapper. He started his career in 2006 alongside Yo Yo Honey Singh in his hip hop group Mafia Mundeer. He is known as India's rapping king and the best rapper in the nation. He did his schooling in Bal Bharti Public School, Pitampura, Delhi.
